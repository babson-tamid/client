What is Tamid?

The TAMID Group is a student-led, non-profit organization, which creates relationships between business-minded students and the Israeli economy/start up nation. Founded in 2008, nearly thirty elite U.S. universities have TAMID on their campuses. TAMID gives students hands on experience with the Israeli start up nation and economy.
